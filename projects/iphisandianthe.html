Iphis and Ianthe

SEPTEMBER 2023 - JUN 2024

Iphis and Ianthe is a short film that places the Greek myth of the same name in a more contemporary framework. The film follows the character Iphis, a transgender boy, who learns acceptance of his body and comfort of sharing himself with another human being.

I took the lead on recording, designing, and editing the audio in the post-production stage for this film. This work was done in Big Sky Studios, and also the multimedia rooms and audio studios of the University of Michigan's Duderstadt Center. Linked below is a trailer for the film:
